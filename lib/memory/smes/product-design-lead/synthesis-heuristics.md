# Product-design-lead synthesis heuristics

## Command Center rebuild

- When product prioritizes operator trust and design recommends pruning IA,
  resolve in favor of removing placeholder destinations from shipped navigation.
- Treat presentation-layer rebuilds as allowed to include bounded service-layer
  repairs when those repairs are necessary for truthful state, live controls, or
  mutation receipts.
- Preserve operator-approved P0 priorities explicitly in synthesized intake:
  staleness repair, home orientation, gate actionability, and receipts.
- Design-canon extension candidates should remain conditional implementation
  options unless the approved experience cannot be expressed with existing
  tokens.
